Barty Social Content

During my time working at Yes Agency I was assigned to edit several videos to be posted on Ash Barty’s LinkedIn to promote her partnership with Optus at the time. Utilising my skills in  After effects and Premiere I enhanced the content through dynamic motion graphics that aligned with Optus’s brand guidelines to mark her partnership in the social media space.

Working on this project independently allowed me to develop my workflow and design process, and significantly improve my Editing ability in Premiere and Motion Graphic skills in After Effects. It was at first a nervous experience being the first project I had full control of myself, But I was lucky to have enough support from my manager and the seniors to give me confidence.

My tasks included color grading, audio mixing, subtitling, transitions, and adding motion graphics to create coherent content for her social media channels. I gained valuable experience in working independently, meeting deadlines, and effectively communicating with clients and team members.
